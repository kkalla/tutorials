Recipe of Rosti

How to Make Swiss Potato Rosti

Reference link

Ingredients

  • 3 to 4 medium starchy potatoes, like russets (peeled)
  • 2 to 4 tablespoons butter
  • salt to taste
  • optional add-ins: parsley, onions, etc.

Cook

  1. Melt 2 to 4 tablesppons of butter in cast-iron skillet
  2. Add the grated potatoes about 1 inch deep and salt them as desired
  3. Add onions or optional spices as desired
  4. Cook the potatoes over medium heat several minutes
  5. Pat the potatoes into a cake with the spatula and let them cook for 10 minutes
  6. Cover the potatoes and cook for another 5 to 10 minutes
